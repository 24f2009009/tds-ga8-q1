## Financial Formula Example (Math)

The discounted cash flow (DCF) valuation formula:

\[
DCF = \sum_{t=1}^{N} \frac{CF_t}{(1+r)^t}
\]

Or the compound annual growth rate (CAGR):

\[
CAGR = \left( \frac{V_f}{V_i} \right)^{1/n} - 1
\]
